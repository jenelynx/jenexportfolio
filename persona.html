👤 User Persona · Marc Aguinaldo

📌 Basic Info 19 years old · Male · College Student Balingog East Guimba, Nueva Ecija
🎯 Goals Finish assignments on time, keep track of school tasks, avoid forgetting deadlines.
⚠️ Pain Points Forgets due dates, loses track of assignments, stressed with many tasks, hard to wake up early.
💪 Motivations Stay on top of schoolwork, feel less stressed, have a clear daily plan.
📱 Technology Smartphone (reminders, notes), laptop (assignments, coding).
🌞 Typical Day Attends classes, notes tasks, works in afternoon but sometimes forgets priorities; checks deadlines at night.
🧩 Needs Clear class schedule, deadline reminders, easy access to notes/files/announcements in one place.

🗺️ User Journey Map

Awareness

Touchpoints: Class announcements, group chat, syllabus.

Emotions: Confused / anxious

Pain Points: Sometimes doesn't know all tasks on time.

Opportunities: Send reminders or notification about new assignments.

Consideration

Touchpoints: Check schedule, class materials, apps.

Emotions: Overwhelmed, stressed

Pain Points: Feels unsure which task to do first.

Opportunities: Provide a simple task list sorted by priority or deadline.

Purchase (Decision)

Touchpoints: Decide which assignment to start.

Emotions: Hesitant, stressed

Pain Points: Hard to choose where to begin.

Opportunities: Suggest daily or weekly plan for tasks.

Use

Touchpoints: Works on assignment using phone or notes.

Emotions: Focused but sometimes tired.

Pain Points: Can forget tasks or lose track of progress.

Opportunities: Connect tasks across devices, give progress tracking.

Support

Touchpoints: Reviews lessons, submits work, checks if done.

Emotions: Relieved but tired.

Pain Points: Difficulty waking up early, remembering tasks.

Opportunities: Include gentle reminders, alerts, or motivational prompts.
