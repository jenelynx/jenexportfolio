☀️ Chapter 1: Summer Fun & Hard Work

In a sunny meadow, Andy the Ant worked hard every day, carrying grains and berries to his cozy home. He hummed a little tune as he prepared for the winter ahead.

Nearby, Gary the Grasshopper loved to sing, dance, and enjoy the warm sunshine. "Andy, why do you work so hard?" Gary laughed.

"Come play with me! The sun is shining!" Andy shook his head. "I must prepare for winter, Gary. When it's cold, we need food to eat."

But Gary just shrugged and continued hopping among the flowers. He spent the summer singing, laughing, and playing.

❄️ Chapter 2: Winter Arrives

Winter came quickly. Snow covered the meadow, and the flowers and berries disappeared.

Gary shivered in the cold. "Oh no! I'm hungry and have nothing to eat!"

Andy stayed warm in his cozy home, enjoying the food he had stored carefully.

Andy kindly invited Gary to share his food, teaching him the value of preparation.

🌤️ Chapter 3: Learning Balance

When the next summer arrived, Gary worked alongside Andy to gather food.

This time, he also took time to sing and play, learning to balance work and fun.

Andy and Gary became best friends, enjoying sunny days and staying prepared for winter.

🌟 Moral: Hard work and planning are important, but joy and fun complete life.
